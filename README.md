This is a two player Tic Tac Toe. First the players will choose who will be X or O
Then they will enter a position number where they want to mark thier X or O (ex: 1,2,3)

The board position numbers are like this: 

1  |  2  |  3
-------------
4  |  5  |  6
-------------
7  |  8  |  9

If you want to stop the game at any certain point just write quit and the game will stop
